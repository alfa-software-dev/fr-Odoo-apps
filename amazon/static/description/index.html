AdlerDo - Amazon & Odoo Integration Connector

Seamlessly connect your Amazon Seller Central account with Odoo. Sync products, orders, shipments & inventory in real-time.

What is AdlerDo for Amazon?

AdlerDo for Amazon is a powerful integration connector that bridges your Amazon Seller Central account with Odoo ERP - creating a unified commerce ecosystem where your marketplace sales and backend operations work in perfect harmony.

From automatic order imports to real-time inventory updates, from product listing management to FBA synchronization - AdlerDo eliminates manual data entry and keeps your Amazon seller account and Odoo perfectly aligned.

Integration Capabilities

  • Amazon SP-API (Selling Partner API)
  • Amazon FBA (Fulfillment by Amazon)
  • Amazon FBM (Fulfillment by Merchant)
  • Amazon Multi-Channel Fulfillment (MCF)
  • Multiple Amazon Marketplaces (EU, NA, FE)
  • Amazon Product Listings & Catalog
  • Amazon Order Management
  • Amazon Messaging & Customer Communication
  • Amazon Reports & Analytics

Key Features

  • Product Listing Sync - Synchronize products, ASINs, SKUs, images, prices, and descriptions from Odoo to Amazon.
  • Real-time Order Import - Automatically import Amazon orders (FBA & FBM) into Odoo with full line item, discount, and tax details.
  • Inventory Management - Keep stock levels synchronized between Amazon and Odoo warehouses in real-time.
  • FBA Inventory Sync - Track and manage your Fulfillment by Amazon inventory directly within Odoo.
  • Shipment & Tracking Updates - Push carrier information and tracking numbers from Odoo to Amazon automatically.
  • Returns & Refunds Processing - Handle Amazon returns and refunds directly within Odoo.
  • Multi-marketplace & Multi-account Support

Who is it for?

AdlerDo for Amazon is made for:

  • Odoo integrators & developers
  • Amazon sellers using Odoo as their ERP
  • E-Commerce agencies managing Amazon clients
  • Retailers expanding into global Amazon marketplaces
  • Businesses seeking unified multichannel operations

Whether you're a new Amazon seller or managing a high-volume catalog across multiple marketplaces, AdlerDo delivers seamless automation and operational efficiency.
