AdlerDo - Zalando & Odoo Integration Connector

Seamlessly connect your Zalando Partner account with Odoo. Sync products, orders, shipments & inventory in real-time.

What is AdlerDo for Zalando?

AdlerDo for Zalando is a powerful integration connector that bridges your Zalando Partner account with Odoo ERP - creating a unified commerce ecosystem where your fashion marketplace sales and backend operations work in perfect harmony.

From automatic order imports to real-time inventory updates, from product listing management to shipment tracking - AdlerDo eliminates manual data entry and keeps your Zalando account and Odoo perfectly aligned.

Integration Capabilities

  • Zalando Partner API (zDirect)
  • Zalando Connected Retail
  • Zalando Fulfillment Solutions (ZFS)
  • Multiple Zalando Markets (DE, FR, IT, NL, BE, PL & more)
  • Product Listing & Article Management
  • Order Management
  • Shipment & Tracking
  • Returns & Refunds
  • Multiple Zalando Accounts

Key Features

  • Product Listing Sync - Synchronize products, sizes, colors, images, and prices from Odoo to Zalando.
  • Real-time Order Import - Automatically import Zalando orders into Odoo with full line item, discount, and tax details.
  • Inventory Management - Keep stock levels synchronized between Zalando and Odoo warehouses in real-time.
  • Shipment & Tracking Updates - Push carrier information and tracking numbers from Odoo to Zalando automatically.
  • Returns & Refunds Processing - Handle Zalando returns and refunds directly within Odoo.
  • Category & Attribute Mapping - Map Zalando silhouettes and attributes to Odoo product categories seamlessly.
  • Multi-market & Multi-account Support
