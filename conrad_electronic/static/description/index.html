AdlerDo - Conrad Electronic & Odoo Integration Connector

Seamlessly connect your Conrad Electronic marketplace seller account with Odoo. Sync products, orders, shipments & inventory in real-time.

What is AdlerDo for Conrad Electronic?

AdlerDo for Conrad Electronic is a powerful integration connector that bridges your Conrad Electronic marketplace seller account with Odoo ERP - creating a unified commerce ecosystem where your electronics marketplace sales and backend operations work in perfect harmony.

From automatic order imports to real-time inventory updates, from product offer management to shipment tracking - AdlerDo eliminates manual data entry and keeps your Conrad Electronic account and Odoo perfectly aligned.

Integration Capabilities

  • Conrad Marketplace API (Mirakl-powered)
  • Conrad Germany
  • Conrad Austria
  • Conrad Switzerland
  • Product Offer Management
  • Order Management
  • Shipment & Tracking
  • Returns & Refunds
  • Multiple Seller Accounts

Key Features

  • Product Offer Sync - Synchronize product offers, EANs, prices, and stock levels from Odoo to Conrad Electronic.
  • Real-time Order Import - Automatically import Conrad Electronic orders into Odoo with full line item, discount, and tax details.
  • Inventory Management - Keep stock levels synchronized between Conrad Electronic and Odoo warehouses in real-time.
  • Shipment & Tracking Updates - Push carrier information and tracking numbers from Odoo to Conrad Electronic automatically.
  • Returns & Refunds Processing - Handle marketplace returns and refunds directly within Odoo.
  • Category & Attribute Mapping - Map Conrad Electronic categories and attributes to Odoo product categories seamlessly.
  • Multi-country & Multi-account Support
