AdlerDo - Kaufland & Odoo Integration Connector

Seamlessly connect your Kaufland Marketplace seller account with Odoo. Sync products, orders, shipments & inventory in real-time.

What is AdlerDo for Kaufland?

AdlerDo for Kaufland is a powerful integration connector that bridges your Kaufland Marketplace seller account with Odoo ERP - creating a unified commerce ecosystem where your marketplace sales and backend operations work in perfect harmony.

From automatic order imports to real-time inventory updates, from product listing management to shipment tracking - AdlerDo eliminates manual data entry and keeps your Kaufland Marketplace account and Odoo perfectly aligned.

Integration Capabilities

  • Kaufland Marketplace API
  • Kaufland.de (Germany)
  • Kaufland.cz (Czech Republic)
  • Kaufland.sk (Slovakia)
  • Kaufland Product Listings & Units
  • Kaufland Order Management
  • Kaufland Shipment & Returns
  • Multiple Kaufland Storefronts
  • Kaufland Categories & Attributes

Key Features

  • Product Listing Sync - Synchronize products, EANs, images, prices, and descriptions from Odoo to Kaufland Marketplace.
  • Real-time Order Import - Automatically import Kaufland orders into Odoo with full line item, discount, and tax details.
  • Inventory Management - Keep stock levels synchronized between Kaufland Marketplace and Odoo warehouses in real-time.
  • Shipment & Tracking Updates - Push carrier information and tracking numbers from Odoo to Kaufland automatically.
  • Returns & Ticket Processing - Handle Kaufland returns and customer tickets directly within Odoo.
  • Category & Attribute Mapping - Map Kaufland categories and attributes to Odoo product categories seamlessly.
  • Multi-storefront & Multi-warehouse Support
