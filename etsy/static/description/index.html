AdlerDo - Etsy & Odoo Integration Connector

Seamlessly connect your Etsy shop with Odoo. Sync products, orders, customers & inventory in real-time.

What is AdlerDo for Etsy?

AdlerDo for Etsy is a powerful integration connector that bridges your Etsy shop with Odoo ERP - creating a unified commerce ecosystem where your handmade & vintage marketplace sales and backend operations work in perfect harmony.

From automatic order imports to real-time inventory updates, from product listing management to shipment tracking - AdlerDo eliminates manual data entry and keeps your Etsy shop and Odoo perfectly aligned.

Integration Capabilities

  • Etsy Open API v3
  • Etsy Global Marketplace
  • Etsy Listings & Variations
  • Etsy Order Management
  • Etsy Shipment & Tracking
  • Etsy Reviews & Messages
  • Etsy Ads & Offsite Ads
  • Multiple Etsy Shops
  • Etsy Pattern Sites

Key Features

  • Product Listing Sync - Synchronize products, variations, images, prices, and descriptions from Odoo to Etsy.
  • Real-time Order Import - Automatically import Etsy orders into Odoo with full line item, discount, and tax details.
  • Inventory Management - Keep stock levels synchronized between Etsy and Odoo warehouses in real-time.
  • Shipment & Tracking Updates - Push carrier information and tracking numbers from Odoo to Etsy automatically.
  • Customer Synchronization - Sync customer data and order history between both platforms.
  • Category & Tag Mapping - Map Etsy categories and tags to Odoo product categories seamlessly.
  • Multi-shop & Multi-currency Support
