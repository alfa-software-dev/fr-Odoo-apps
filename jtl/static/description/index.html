AdlerDo - JTL & Odoo Integration Connector

Seamlessly connect JTL-Wawi & JTL-Shop with Odoo. Sync products, orders, customers & inventory in real-time.

What is AdlerDo for JTL?

AdlerDo for JTL is a powerful integration connector that bridges your JTL ecosystem with Odoo ERP - creating a unified commerce ecosystem where your warehouse management, online sales, and backend operations work in perfect harmony.

From automatic order synchronization to real-time inventory updates, from product data management to customer synchronization - AdlerDo eliminates manual data entry and keeps your JTL-Wawi, JTL-Shop, and Odoo perfectly aligned.

Integration Capabilities

  • JTL-Wawi (Warehouse Management)
  • JTL-Shop 4 & 5
  • JTL-WMS (Warehouse Management System)
  • JTL-Fulfillment Network
  • JTL-Connector & REST API
  • JTL-eazyAuction (eBay & Amazon)
  • JTL-POS (Point of Sale)
  • JTL Ameise (Import/Export)
  • Multiple JTL-Wawi Mandanten

Key Features

  • Bi-directional Product Sync - Synchronize products, variants, images, prices, and descriptions between JTL-Wawi and Odoo.
  • Real-time Order Import - Automatically import JTL orders into Odoo with full line item, discount, and tax details.
  • Inventory Management - Keep stock levels synchronized across JTL-Wawi warehouses and Odoo in real-time.
  • Customer Synchronization - Sync customer data, addresses, and order history between both platforms.
  • Fulfillment & Shipping Updates - Push tracking numbers and fulfillment status between JTL and Odoo automatically.
  • Category & Attribute Mapping - Map JTL categories and attributes to Odoo product categories seamlessly.
  • Multi-Mandant & Multi-warehouse Support

Who is it for?

AdlerDo for JTL is made for:

  • Odoo integrators & developers
  • JTL-Wawi users expanding to Odoo ERP
  • E-Commerce agencies managing JTL & Odoo clients
  • Retailers migrating or bridging between JTL and Odoo
  • Businesses seeking unified warehouse and ERP operations

Whether you're running JTL-Wawi alongside Odoo or planning a migration, AdlerDo delivers seamless automation and operational efficiency.
