AdlerDo - Denher & Odoo Integration Connector

Seamlessly connect your Denher supplier platform with Odoo. Sync products, orders, shipments & inventory in real-time.

What is AdlerDo for Denher?

AdlerDo for Denher is a powerful integration connector that bridges your Denher supplier platform with Odoo ERP - creating a unified commerce ecosystem where your supplier data, dropshipping operations, and backend systems work in perfect harmony.

From automatic product imports to real-time inventory updates, from order routing to shipment tracking - AdlerDo eliminates manual data entry and keeps your Denher supplier connections and Odoo perfectly aligned.

Integration Capabilities

  • Denher Supplier API
  • Product Catalog Import
  • Price & Availability Sync
  • Dropshipping Order Routing
  • Order Management
  • Shipment & Tracking
  • Returns & Refunds
  • Multiple Supplier Accounts
  • Category & Product Mapping

Key Features

  • Product Catalog Sync - Import and synchronize product catalogs, images, prices, and descriptions from Denher to Odoo.
  • Real-time Stock Updates - Automatically update stock levels and availability from Denher suppliers in Odoo.
  • Dropshipping Order Routing - Route orders automatically from Odoo to Denher suppliers for fulfillment.
  • Shipment & Tracking Updates - Receive carrier information and tracking numbers from Denher into Odoo automatically.
  • Price Management - Sync supplier pricing and apply markup rules within Odoo.
  • Returns Processing - Handle supplier returns and refunds directly within Odoo.
  • Multi-supplier Support
