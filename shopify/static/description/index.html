AdlerDo - Shopify & Odoo Integration Connector

Seamlessly connect your Shopify store with Odoo. Sync products, orders, customers & inventory in real-time.

What is AdlerDo for Shopify?

AdlerDo for Shopify is a powerful integration connector that bridges your Shopify storefront with Odoo ERP - creating a unified commerce ecosystem where your online sales and backend operations work in perfect harmony.

From automatic order imports to real-time inventory updates, from customer synchronization to fulfillment tracking - AdlerDo eliminates manual data entry and keeps your Shopify store and Odoo perfectly aligned.

Integration Capabilities

  • Shopify Online Store (all plans)
  • Shopify Plus
  • Shopify POS
  • Multiple Shopify Stores
  • Shopify Markets (International)
  • Shopify B2B
  • Third-party Shopify Apps & Plugins
  • Custom Shopify Themes & Metafields

Key Features

  • Bi-directional Product Sync - Synchronize products, variants, images, prices, and descriptions between Shopify and Odoo.
  • Real-time Order Import - Automatically import Shopify orders into Odoo with full line item, discount, and tax details.
  • Inventory Management - Keep stock levels synchronized across Shopify and Odoo warehouses in real-time.
  • Customer Synchronization - Sync customer data, addresses, and order history between both platforms.
  • Fulfillment & Shipping Updates - Push tracking numbers and fulfillment status from Odoo back to Shopify.
  • Webhook-based Updates - Instant synchronization using Shopify webhooks for orders, products, and inventory changes.
  • Multi-location & Multi-currency Support
