AdlerDo - Rakuten & Odoo Integration Connector

Seamlessly connect your Rakuten seller account with Odoo. Sync products, orders, shipments & inventory in real-time.

What is AdlerDo for Rakuten?

AdlerDo for Rakuten is a powerful integration connector that bridges your Rakuten seller account with Odoo ERP - creating a unified commerce ecosystem where your marketplace sales and backend operations work in perfect harmony.

From automatic order imports to real-time inventory updates, from product listing management to shipment tracking - AdlerDo eliminates manual data entry and keeps your Rakuten account and Odoo perfectly aligned.

Integration Capabilities

  • Rakuten Marketplace API
  • Rakuten Germany
  • Rakuten France
  • Rakuten Japan
  • Product Listing Management
  • Order Management
  • Shipment & Tracking
  • Returns & Refunds
  • Multiple Rakuten Accounts

Key Features

  • Product Listing Sync - Synchronize products, EANs, images, prices, and descriptions from Odoo to Rakuten.
  • Real-time Order Import - Automatically import Rakuten orders into Odoo with full line item, discount, and tax details.
  • Inventory Management - Keep stock levels synchronized between Rakuten and Odoo warehouses in real-time.
  • Shipment & Tracking Updates - Push carrier information and tracking numbers from Odoo to Rakuten automatically.
  • Returns & Refunds Processing - Handle Rakuten returns and refunds directly within Odoo.
  • Category & Attribute Mapping - Map Rakuten categories and attributes to Odoo product categories seamlessly.
  • Multi-marketplace & Multi-account Support
