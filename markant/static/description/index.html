AdlerDo - Markant & Odoo Integration Connector

Seamlessly connect your Markant EDI systems with Odoo. Sync products, orders, invoices & inventory in real-time.

What is AdlerDo for Markant?

AdlerDo for Markant is a powerful integration connector that bridges your Markant EDI systems with Odoo ERP - creating a unified commerce ecosystem where your B2B supply chain operations and backend systems work in perfect harmony.

From automatic order imports to real-time inventory updates, from product data management to invoice exchange - AdlerDo eliminates manual data entry and keeps your Markant EDI connections and Odoo perfectly aligned.

Integration Capabilities

  • Markant EDI (EDIFACT / EANCOM)
  • Markant Product Data Management
  • ORDERS (Purchase Orders)
  • DESADV (Dispatch Advice)
  • INVOIC (Invoices)
  • PRICAT (Price Catalog)
  • INVRPT (Inventory Report)
  • Multiple Trading Partners
  • Retail & FMCG Supply Chain

Key Features

  • Product Data Sync - Synchronize product master data, GTINs, prices, and attributes between Markant and Odoo.
  • Real-time Order Import - Automatically import Markant EDI orders into Odoo with full line item and delivery details.
  • Inventory Management - Keep stock levels synchronized between Markant partners and Odoo warehouses in real-time.
  • Dispatch & Tracking Updates - Generate and send DESADV messages from Odoo to Markant partners automatically.
  • Invoice Exchange - Create and exchange electronic invoices (INVOIC) between Odoo and Markant partners.
  • GLN & GTIN Management - Manage Global Location Numbers and Trade Item Numbers seamlessly within Odoo.
  • Multi-partner & Multi-format Support

Who is it for?

AdlerDo for Markant is made for:

  • Suppliers & manufacturers connected to Markant
  • FMCG & consumer goods companies
  • Retail supply chain partners
  • Businesses seeking unified B2B EDI operations
