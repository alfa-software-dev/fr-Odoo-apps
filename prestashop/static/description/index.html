AdlerDo - PrestaShop & Odoo Integration Connector

Seamlessly connect your PrestaShop store with Odoo. Sync products, orders, customers & inventory in real-time.

What is AdlerDo for PrestaShop?

AdlerDo for PrestaShop is a powerful integration connector that bridges your PrestaShop webshop with Odoo ERP - creating a unified commerce ecosystem where your online sales and backend operations work in perfect harmony.

From automatic order imports to real-time inventory updates, from customer synchronization to fulfillment tracking - AdlerDo eliminates manual data entry and keeps your PrestaShop store and Odoo perfectly aligned.

Integration Capabilities

  • PrestaShop 1.6, 1.7 & 8.x
  • ThirtyBees
  • Multiple PrestaShop Stores
  • PrestaShop Multistore
  • Multi-language & Multi-currency Shops
  • PrestaShop Modules & Plugins
  • Custom PrestaShop Themes & Fields
  • PrestaShop Webservice API

Key Features

  • Bi-directional Product Sync - Synchronize products, combinations, images, prices, and descriptions between PrestaShop and Odoo.
  • Real-time Order Import - Automatically import PrestaShop orders into Odoo with full line item, discount, and tax details.
  • Inventory Management - Keep stock levels synchronized across PrestaShop and Odoo warehouses in real-time.
  • Customer Synchronization - Sync customer data, addresses, and order history between both platforms.
  • Fulfillment & Shipping Updates - Push tracking numbers and fulfillment status from Odoo back to PrestaShop.
  • Category & Attribute Mapping - Map PrestaShop categories and attributes to Odoo product categories seamlessly.
  • Multi-shop & Multi-language Support

Who is it for?

AdlerDo for PrestaShop is made for:

  • Odoo integrators & developers
  • PrestaShop merchants using Odoo as their ERP
  • E-Commerce agencies managing PrestaShop clients
  • Retailers migrating or expanding their online presence
  • Businesses seeking unified commerce operations

Whether you're running a single PrestaShop store or managing multiple multishop instances, AdlerDo delivers seamless automation and operational efficiency.
