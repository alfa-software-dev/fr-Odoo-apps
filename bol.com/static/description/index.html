AdlerDo - Bol.com & Odoo Integration Connector

Seamlessly connect your Bol.com seller account with Odoo. Sync products, orders, shipments & inventory in real-time.

What is AdlerDo for Bol.com?

AdlerDo for Bol.com is a powerful integration connector that bridges your Bol.com seller account with Odoo ERP - creating a unified commerce ecosystem where your marketplace sales and backend operations work in perfect harmony.

From automatic order imports to real-time inventory updates, from product offer management to shipment tracking - AdlerDo eliminates manual data entry and keeps your Bol.com account and Odoo perfectly aligned.

Integration Capabilities

  • Bol.com Retailer API v10
  • Bol.com Netherlands
  • Bol.com Belgium
  • Bol.com FBB (Fulfillment by Bol.com)
  • Bol.com FBR (Fulfillment by Retailer)
  • Product Offer Management
  • Order Management
  • Shipment & Tracking
  • Returns & Refunds

Key Features

  • Product Offer Sync - Synchronize product offers, EANs, prices, and stock levels from Odoo to Bol.com.
  • Real-time Order Import - Automatically import Bol.com orders into Odoo with full line item, discount, and tax details.
  • Inventory Management - Keep stock levels synchronized between Bol.com and Odoo warehouses in real-time.
  • Shipment & Tracking Updates - Push carrier information and tracking numbers from Odoo to Bol.com automatically.
  • Returns & Refunds Processing - Handle Bol.com returns and refunds directly within Odoo.
  • FBB Inventory Sync - Track and manage your Fulfillment by Bol.com inventory directly within Odoo.
  • Multi-country & Multi-account Support
