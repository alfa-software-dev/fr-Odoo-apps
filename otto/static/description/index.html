AdlerDo - Otto Market & Odoo Integration Connector

Seamlessly connect your Otto Market seller account with Odoo. Sync products, orders, shipments & inventory in real-time.

What is AdlerDo for Otto Market?

AdlerDo for Otto Market is a powerful integration connector that bridges your Otto Market seller account with Odoo ERP - creating a unified commerce ecosystem where your marketplace sales and backend operations work in perfect harmony.

From automatic order imports to real-time inventory updates, from product listing management to shipment tracking - AdlerDo eliminates manual data entry and keeps your Otto Market account and Odoo perfectly aligned.

Integration Capabilities

  • Otto Market Partner API
  • Otto Market Product Listings
  • Otto Market Order Management
  • Otto Market Shipment & Returns
  • Otto Market Receipts & Invoicing
  • Multiple Otto Market Accounts
  • Otto Market Categories & Attributes
  • Otto Market Brand Management

Key Features

  • Product Listing Sync - Synchronize products, variants, images, prices, and descriptions from Odoo to Otto Market.
  • Real-time Order Import - Automatically import Otto Market orders into Odoo with full line item, discount, and tax details.
  • Inventory Management - Keep stock levels synchronized between Otto Market and Odoo warehouses in real-time.
  • Shipment & Tracking Updates - Push carrier information and tracking numbers from Odoo to Otto Market automatically.
  • Returns Processing - Handle Otto Market returns and refunds directly within Odoo.
  • Category & Attribute Mapping - Map Otto Market categories and attributes to Odoo product categories seamlessly.
  • Multi-account & Multi-warehouse Support
