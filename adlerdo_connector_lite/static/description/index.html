AdlerDo - Multichannel E-Commerce & EDI Middleware for Odoo

Boost your E-Commerce with seamless integrations to Amazon, eBay, Kaufland, MediaMarkt, Otto & more. Built for Odoo.

What is AdlerDo?

AdlerDo is a modular middleware solution for Odoo that connects your ERP system to leading marketplaces, suppliers, and fulfillment networks (FFN) - automating every step of your multichannel E-Commerce and EDI workflows.

From product listings to stock synchronization, from order routing to supplier onboarding - AdlerDo brings everything into one centralized, scalable system, fully integrated with Odoo.

Supported Platforms & Channels

  • Amazon (FBA & FBM)
  • eBay
  • Kaufland.de
  • MediaMarkt & Saturn (via EDI)
  • Otto Market
  • Allegro
  • FFN (Fulfillment Networks)
  • Supplier systems (CSV, XML, API, FTP)
  • Custom third-party stores via API or EDI

Key Features

  • Multichannel Product Sync - Automatically synchronize product listings, prices, stock levels, and categories.
  • Real-time Order Flow - Import, route, and update orders from multiple marketplaces - directly into Odoo.
  • Supplier & EDI Integration - Automate catalog imports, availability, and pricing from suppliers. Full EDI support for B2B partners.
  • Pricing & VAT Automation - Custom pricing rules, multi-country VAT logic, and shipping methods per channel.
  • Dropshipping & Stock Sync - Connect to suppliers and fulfillment partners for automatic dropship workflows.
  • Advanced Mapping & Logs - Full control with mapping tables, transformation rules, and technical logging.
  • Multi-language & Multi-currency Ready
