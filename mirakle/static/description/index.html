What is AdlerDo for Mirakl?

AdlerDo for Mirakl is a powerful integration connector that bridges Mirakl-powered marketplaces with Odoo ERP - creating a unified commerce ecosystem where your marketplace sales and backend operations work in perfect harmony.

From automatic order imports to real-time inventory updates, from product offer management to shipment tracking - AdlerDo eliminates manual data entry and keeps your Mirakl marketplace accounts and Odoo perfectly aligned.

Key Features

  • Product Offer Sync - Synchronize product offers, prices, stock levels, and descriptions from Odoo to Mirakl marketplaces.
  • Real-time Order Import - Automatically import Mirakl orders into Odoo with full line item, discount, and tax details.
  • Inventory Management - Keep stock levels synchronized between Mirakl marketplaces and Odoo warehouses in real-time.
  • Shipment & Tracking Updates - Push carrier information and tracking numbers from Odoo to Mirakl automatically.
  • Returns & Refunds Processing - Handle Mirakl returns and refunds directly within Odoo.
  • Category & Attribute Mapping - Map Mirakl marketplace categories and attributes to Odoo product categories seamlessly.
  • Multi-marketplace & Multi-account Support
