AdlerDo - Walmart & Odoo Integration Connector

Seamlessly connect your Walmart Marketplace seller account with Odoo. Sync products, orders, shipments & inventory in real-time.

What is AdlerDo for Walmart?

AdlerDo for Walmart is a powerful integration connector that bridges your Walmart Marketplace seller account with Odoo ERP - creating a unified commerce ecosystem where your marketplace sales and backend operations work in perfect harmony.

From automatic order imports to real-time inventory updates, from product listing management to shipment tracking - AdlerDo eliminates manual data entry and keeps your Walmart Marketplace account and Odoo perfectly aligned.

Integration Capabilities

  • Walmart Marketplace API
  • Walmart US Marketplace
  • Walmart Canada
  • Walmart Mexico
  • Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS)
  • Product Listing Management
  • Order Management
  • Shipment & Tracking
  • Returns & Refunds

Key Features

  • Product Listing Sync - Synchronize products, SKUs, UPCs, images, prices, and descriptions from Odoo to Walmart.
  • Real-time Order Import - Automatically import Walmart orders into Odoo with full line item, discount, and tax details.
  • Inventory Management - Keep stock levels synchronized between Walmart and Odoo warehouses in real-time.
  • WFS Inventory Sync - Track and manage your Walmart Fulfillment Services inventory directly within Odoo.
  • Shipment & Tracking Updates - Push carrier information and tracking numbers from Odoo to Walmart automatically.
  • Returns & Refunds Processing - Handle Walmart returns and refunds directly within Odoo.
  • Multi-marketplace & Multi-account Support

AdlerDo for Walmart is made for:

  • Walmart Marketplace sellers using Odoo as their ERP
  • E-Commerce agencies managing North American clients
