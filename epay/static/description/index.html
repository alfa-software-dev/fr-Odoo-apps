AdlerDo - eBay & Odoo Integration Connector

Seamlessly connect your eBay seller account with Odoo. Sync products, orders, shipments & inventory in real-time.

What is AdlerDo for eBay?

AdlerDo for eBay is a powerful integration connector that bridges your eBay seller account with Odoo ERP - creating a unified commerce ecosystem where your marketplace sales and backend operations work in perfect harmony.

From automatic order imports to real-time inventory updates, from product listing management to shipment tracking - AdlerDo eliminates manual data entry and keeps your eBay seller account and Odoo perfectly aligned.

Integration Capabilities

  • eBay REST API & Browse API
  • eBay Fulfillment API
  • eBay Inventory API
  • Multiple eBay Marketplaces (US, UK, DE, FR, IT, ES, AU & more)
  • eBay Fixed Price & Auction Listings
  • eBay Order Management
  • eBay Messaging & Buyer Communication
  • eBay Managed Payments
  • eBay Promoted Listings

Key Features

  • Product Listing Sync - Synchronize products, item specifics, images, prices, and descriptions from Odoo to eBay.
  • Real-time Order Import - Automatically import eBay orders into Odoo with full line item, discount, and tax details.
  • Inventory Management - Keep stock levels synchronized between eBay and Odoo warehouses in real-time.
  • Shipment & Tracking Updates - Push carrier information and tracking numbers from Odoo to eBay automatically.
  • Returns & Refunds Processing - Handle eBay returns and refunds directly within Odoo.
  • Category & Item Specifics Mapping - Map eBay categories and item specifics to Odoo product attributes seamlessly.
  • Multi-marketplace & Multi-account Support

Who is it for?

AdlerDo for eBay is made for:

  • Odoo integrators & developers
  • eBay sellers using Odoo as their ERP
  • E-Commerce agencies managing eBay clients
  • Retailers expanding into global eBay marketplaces
  • Businesses seeking unified multichannel operations

Whether you're a new eBay seller or managing a high-volume catalog across multiple marketplaces, AdlerDo delivers seamless automation and operational efficiency.
