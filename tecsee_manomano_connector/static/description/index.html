AdlerDo - ManoMano & Odoo Integration Connector

Seamlessly connect your ManoMano seller account with Odoo. Sync products, orders, shipments & inventory in real-time.

What is AdlerDo for ManoMano?

AdlerDo for ManoMano is a powerful integration connector that bridges your ManoMano seller account with Odoo ERP - creating a unified commerce ecosystem where your DIY & home improvement marketplace sales and backend operations work in perfect harmony.

From automatic order imports to real-time inventory updates, from product offer management to shipment tracking - AdlerDo eliminates manual data entry and keeps your ManoMano account and Odoo perfectly aligned.

Integration Capabilities

  • ManoMano Seller API (Mirakl-powered)
  • ManoMano France
  • ManoMano Germany
  • ManoMano Spain
  • ManoMano Italy
  • ManoMano UK
  • Product Offer Management
  • Order Management
  • Shipment & Tracking
  • Returns & Refunds

Key Features

  • Product Offer Sync - Synchronize product offers, EANs, prices, and stock levels from Odoo to ManoMano.
  • Real-time Order Import - Automatically import ManoMano orders into Odoo with full line item, discount, and tax details.
  • Inventory Management - Keep stock levels synchronized between ManoMano and Odoo warehouses in real-time.
  • Shipment & Tracking Updates - Push carrier information and tracking numbers from Odoo to ManoMano automatically.
  • Returns & Refunds Processing - Handle ManoMano returns and refunds directly within Odoo.
  • Category & Attribute Mapping - Map ManoMano categories and attributes to Odoo product categories seamlessly.
  • Multi-country & Multi-account Support
